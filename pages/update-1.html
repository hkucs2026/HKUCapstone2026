Data Collection

Summary of source coverage and curation process

Objective

Establish a reliable and authoritative knowledge base to support home maintenance and repair guidance in the mobile app.

Trusted Data Sources Collected

  • Government and Official Bodies (Hong Kong): Buildings Department guidelines on drainage, water seepage, window safety, and building inspection.
  • Joint FEHD and Buildings Department materials for DIY water seepage testing.
  • Consumer Council (Hong Kong): appliance maintenance, usage tips, safety guidance, and energy saving advice across major household appliances.
  • Overseas authoritative references: Australian Government (YourHome) guidance on residential repairs and maintenance.
  • Reputable DIY and repair websites: step-by-step home repair, plumbing, cleaning, and indoor environment resources.
  • Manufacturer user manuals: official manuals covering washing machines, refrigerators, air conditioners, TVs, and vacuum cleaners from major brands.

Key Knowledge Domains Covered

  • Building maintenance and safety: drainage and water seepage, window inspection and safety compliance, structural maintenance and defect awareness.
  • Home appliance knowledge: maintenance, cleaning, proper usage, purchasing considerations, energy efficiency, electricity cost reduction, and appliance safety risks.
  • DIY repair and home environment: general DIY repairs and improvements, plumbing troubleshooting (for example leaks and clogged drains), cleaning, mold prevention, and humidity control.
  • Primary source documentation: manufacturer manuals for accurate operation, specifications, and troubleshooting.
